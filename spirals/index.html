About Spiral Tilings

Spiral tilings are a fascinating class of tilings where the tiles are arranged in spiral patterns around central points. These tilings can create beautiful, hypnotic patterns reminiscent of M.C. Escher's work.

This interactive tool allows you to experiment with different tiling types and spiral parameters to create your own unique spiral patterns.
